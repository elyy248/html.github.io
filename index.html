FACILITIES

  • External Look of Tamarind Hall

    • Tamarind Hall, in short Tama Hall, has a very short heritage especially since it is established in 2017. It is located within the Nanyang Crescent Halls cluster. New halls like Tama Hall, have a wide range of new facilities and services for residents to use.





  • Internal Amenities and Services

    • Inside the hall, we have

    - The Lounge for students to use for relaxation purposes and group discussions.
    - The Reading Room where hall residents can study in this quiet and conducive environment.
    - The Laundry Room for residents to wash and dry their clothes.
    - The Gym for students to visit when they need a workout and exercise break from study.
    - The Toilets which will be the most commonly visited place residents visit when they are in their rooms. It is also conveniently located for easy access.

    ...and many more!
